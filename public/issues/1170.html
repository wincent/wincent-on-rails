Description

I am running the latest edition of iTunes with Leopard. The box for providing 'extra visual feedback' is not checked, but i still get the notifications every time i pause or play a song, even when i use the normal iTunes controls and not the hotkeys. I have tried reverting to the default settings and re-checking and unchecking the box again, but nothing takes away the extra feedback.
