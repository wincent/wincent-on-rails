Feature request #2142: Consider replacing tweets feature with deeper Twitter integration

Kind feature request
Product wincent.com
When Created 2014-01-11T22:58:54Z, updated 2014-01-12T02:01:07Z
Status closed
Reporter Greg Hurrell
Tags no tags

Description

When I originally added the tweets feature, I didn't care to integrate with Twitter (back in the early days it was unreliable and I scarcely saw the point of it). Nevertheless, I wanted the ability to post short remarks that were of tweet-length, so I built it.

Later on I added actual integration, but it's currently bombing out (see ticket #2134). I could fix that, but I think the time now is to re-evaluate the existence of this feature. Twitter is now reliable (it's a publicly-traded company at least), so while it may not be around forever, the likelihood of it remaining into the foreseeable future is considerable. And on the flip side, building and maintaining my own model is non-free. Additionally, there's a network effect at Twitter that increases the value of it as a distribution channel beyond the technicalities of it as an implementation.

So, this ticket is to suggest that it might be worth removing the old tweet model (the data could be transferred over to snippets for posterity), and replacing it with a "deeper" integration in which I just post my tweets on Twitter directly, and mirror them over here, instead of the other way around.
