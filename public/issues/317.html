Bug #317: Notiification Bezel looks horrible when fully transparent

Kind bug
Product Synergy Advance
When 2005-10-22T19:52:33Z
Status closed
Reporter John Davidorff Pell
Tags no tags

Description

Synergy Advance puts up the little play/pause/whatever bezel in the style of the vol up/down or screen brightness thingies. Apparently, synergy does not put up a new window, but rather keeps a window in place and transparent... This window makes drawing in only that little square EXTREMELY slow on my Mac Mini and iBook... This means that scrolling in Safari has a square artefact for fractions of every second.

Thanks! JP
