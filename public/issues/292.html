Comments

  1. Greg Hurrell 2005-09-07T23:38:46Z

    I'm not sure I understand the question. Growl notifications are plain text only with no formatting information. Synergy can control what text gets sent but it can't control how Growl displays it. You control the appearance of the Growl notification using the Growl preference pane (ie. go to System Preferences and then click on the Growl preference pane). Synergy and Synergy Advance don't (and can't) send font and formatting information to Growl.

    In contrast Synergy's own Floater can be customized, of course. The Floater module for Synergy Advance hasn't been released yet, but it will be.
