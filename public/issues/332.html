Description

Thanks for making this feature what it should be... I'll admit I haven't downloaded your product yet, but I haven't seen what I MOST want in the screenshots and features descriptions.

(Perhaps it's there and I missed it? If so, please forgive me for wasting your time!)

I think the most glaring omission from the Fast User Switching menu is a "Log Out" menu item. Yeah, I know it's in the Apple menu, but it really SHOULD be part of the Fast User Switching function! It makes sense to see who-all is logged in AND be able to log out on the same menu!
