Feature request #316: REQUEST: Hot key for "Play Party Shuffle" or "Switch to Pary Shuffle"

Kind feature request
Product Synergy Advance
When Created 2005-10-22T14:35:38Z, updated 2006-07-05T02:06:43Z
Status open
Reporter John Davidorff Pell
Tags no tags

Description

(Great product)

I almost never play anything besides Party Shuffle, but do have many playlist for (a) the times when I want to look for something extremely specific and for (b) my iPod. If I had no playlists, hitting play when I come home without looking at iTunes (by hitting the hot key for Synergy, for example) will almost definitely start party shuffle. But, because I have so many, on the off-chance I had a different one selected when I left, when I hit play it may be start some obscure song, or even a video. I propose that there be some option that allows me to either tell Synergy to only work with Party Shuffle, or so that I can assign a hot key to "Play Party Shuffle". :-)

Thanks!

JP
