  2. Greg Hurrell 2007-03-27T11:17:53Z

    The most common reasons why Synergy will not launch at login include one or more of following:

    a. You might not be using the latest version

    b. Your "login items" might be out-of-date, and pointing at an older version of Synergy

    c. You might have installed a new version of Synergy, but you left the old version on the system as well.

    d. Independently, your login items might not be corrupt, out-of-date or stale.

    So, the way to get Synergy to launch at login is therefore:

    1. Ensure you have installed the latest version of Synergy. Make sure you have not left any old copies of the software in another location on your system (for example, in /Library/PreferencePanes/ or ~/Library/PreferencePanes/).

    2. In the Synergy preferences, un-check the "Launch at login" preference and click "Apply", then re-check it and click "Apply". This forces any old or stale entries in your login items to be updated.
