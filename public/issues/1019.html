Comments

  1. Greg Hurrell 2008-07-27T20:10:47Z

    I don't think it's a bug. The most likely cause is that your login items are out of date. Try forcing an update by:

    1. Unchecking the "Launch at login" checkbox
    2. Clicking "Apply"
    3. Rechecking the checkbox
    4. Clicking "Apply"

    If that doesn't work, please let me know.
