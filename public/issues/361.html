Comments

  1. Greg Hurrell 2006-02-05T19:19:15Z

    Thanks for submitting the request.

    At the moment Synergy Advance explicitly performs alphabetical sorting in the upper levels of the menus (for example, albums in the artists submenu are sorted alphabetically; genres are sorted alphabetically). The playlists in the playlists menu are not sorted but are displayed in the order in which iTunes lists them.

    Likewise, the ordering of tracks at the lowest level of the menus is not explicitly sorted; rather Synergy Advance just shows the tracks in the order in which iTunes provides the information to Synergy Advance.

    To implement what you request properly I think it should actually be a choice of options from a pop-up menu:

    1. Sorted alphabetically (easiest to implement)

    2. Sorted in play-order (will only work for tracks which have track number information)

    3. Unsorted (equivalent to current implementation)
