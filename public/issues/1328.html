Support ticket #1328: Can't get rid of the 2nd of two floaters

Kind support ticket
Product Synergy
When 2009-06-24T00:14:59Z
Status closed
Reporter anonymous
Tags no tags

Description

There are two floaters on my screen that appear whenever a song changes in itunes - one on the lower right corner that I pinned there, and one in the upper right corner that I did not pin nor want appearing. I looked through the settings and I cannot find how to disable it. Hovering over this floater gives an 'x' that you can click and the floater disappears, otherwise it disappears after a few seconds. Also, clicking on this floater before it disappears brings up itunes. As I said, I am trying to disable this floater and cannot find where its preferences are located.
