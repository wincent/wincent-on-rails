Bug #253: iTunes videos break album art display

Kind bug
Product Synergy
When Created 2005-06-08T00:10:52Z, updated 2006-07-05T02:06:00Z
Status open
Reporter bugzilla user 206
Tags no tags

Description

I've been purchasing some of the video content on the iTunes Music Store and noticed a small bug. It seems that when there are videos or cover art PDFs with an album, iTunes will sort them to the top of the list. I've tried changing track numbers to no avail, the extra content always floats to the top.

As a side effect of this, it seems that Synergy pulls the cover art from these files and displays it for every track on the album. So the new Coldplay album purchased from the iTMS has a black bar where its cover art should be.

Perhaps Synergy should ignore that stuff?
