Comments

  1. Greg Hurrell 2005-08-19T21:31:50Z

    The two notifications (both Growl notifications) are coming from two sources:

    1. The top one in the screenshot is coming from Synergy Advance. It has no art because there is not art embedded in the track.

    2. The bottom one in the screenshot is coming from Synergy. It has art because Synergy has downloaded it for you and stored it in your Library folder.

    The solution is to go to your Growl preferences (in the System Preferences application) and turn off notifications for either Synergy or Synergy Advance. Synergy Advance provides more customizability over the formatting of notifications, but it (doesn't yet) know how to find the art already downloaded by Synergy. In the future I will be adding the ability to Synergy Advance to read/find the album art already downloaded by Synergy.
