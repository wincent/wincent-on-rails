  1. Greg Hurrell 2008-04-16T09:38:19Z

    The most common cause of this is out-of-date entries in your login items. You can force a refresh by doing the following:

    1. Uncheck the "launch at login" checkbox
    2. Click "Apply"
    3. Recheck the checkbox
    4. Click "Apply"

    I've got code in 3.5 to automatically check for stale items at launch time, so this problem should arise much less often in the future, but I don't recommend that you upgrade to 3.5 yet because it is still in alpha and there are some known crashing issues.

    Let me know if the above recipe fixes your problem.
