How to clear artwork cache in Synergy

  1. scokim 2006-01-16T03:32:08Z

    I've made a correction to the artwork in iTunes but Synergy still displays the previous incorrect artwork. How can I correct this ? Is there a way to clear what's retrieved by Synergy ?

    Thank you in advance.

  2. Greg Hurrell 2006-01-16T21:05:24Z

    When the track is playing choose "Show Cover Art Folder" from the Synergy Global Menu. The folder will open in the Finder and the offending file will be selected. Delete it.

    The next time you play the song Synergy will use the art embedded using iTunes instead of the incorrect art it previously downloaded.
