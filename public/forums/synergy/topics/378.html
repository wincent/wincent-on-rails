  2. Greg Hurrell 2008-07-02T06:47:34Z

    I expect that one of the notifications you're seeing is the Synergy floater (controllable from the Synergy preferences) and the other is probably a Growl notification (to turn that off you'd have to go in to the Growl preference pane in the System Preferences).

    I guess from your description that the one you want to turn off is the Growl one. Let me know if you can't figure out how to do that.
