  2. Greg Hurrell 2004-06-29T14:07:07Z

    I've heard the same from other users of Volume Logic. I am not sure what it's doing to iTunes' ability to respond to Apple Events.

    Synergy Advance won't be affected if iTunes is blocking and not responding to Apple Events, because it's got much better separation between the user interface thread and the other threads in the program. The UI should remain responsive no matter what.
