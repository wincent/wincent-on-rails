    On my desktop, the Yahoo! "iTunes Remote" widget works alright. Can't configure what to display, and can't resize window, so only displays a small part of long song titles. And no ratings, comments, or anything other than title, artist and album. And only the first few chars of each if long. Sucks... Previous/next/pause/play/volume are all good, and a shuffle/non-shuffle toggle is good. Nothing else good to say about it. No hot keys. No configuration.     Speaking the titles is an unexpectedly awesome thing however. I've been travelling for a few days now with my laptop on the seat of the car and running iTunes out thru a cassette adaptor into my car stereo. With the lid closed and the screen off, it goes several hours on an old battery. Although I have a cig. lighter power cord for "long" trips, I haven't had to use it yet. It is surprisingly cool to have the songs announced, giveing me my own radio station which plays only my own CDs; awesome!

    But the voice is awful and the configuration possibilities are nill. If you implement this, please include these things (not in the Yahoo! widget):

    It speaks OVER the first few seconds of the track, so you can't hear the beginning of the track. I want to control when it speaks, for instance, to speak completely before playing the track so no interference, or possibly speak most of it before and then fade in the track, etc. (much like floater timing sliders now, with relative volumes of announcement vs song being like opacity). I also wish I could configure which fields it speaks and in what order. Also, this widget won't change to other voices, and PC voices are *awful*. Should at least allow config of which voice.

    I'm still looking for a better Windoze program. It is odd -- I've been told (by friends) that there is way more software for pc than mac. But all the software I've actually cared about, google searching for, turns up lots more mac than pc. .. Like iTunes control programs, album cover art searchers, etc...
