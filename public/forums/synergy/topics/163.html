  2. Greg Hurrell 2006-07-29T10:34:56Z

    The album art searches occur in the background and are not instantaneous. Basically what happens is queries are placed in a queue and periodically fired off to the Amazon servers. This is necessary because in the past Amazon has complained about the number of queries being generated by Synergy users, given that no one ever uses the "Buy from Amazon" item in the Global menu. In fact, they even blocked access to Amazon for a period of a few months back in 2003, breaking the cover downloads feature and requiring me to make the changes.

    So what this means in practice is that you may not notice album art arriving in real time, but the next time you play a track you might well find that the album art has been downloaded.

    The is a bug report in the bugs database for the way in which you can skip tracks forwards/backwards to force Synergy to recognize the arrival of art that's been downloaded in the background:

    http://wincent.com/a/support/bugs/show_bug.cgi?id=94

    This should really happen automatically so I am going to try and get it fixed for version 3.0.
