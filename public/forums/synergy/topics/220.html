  2. Greg Hurrell 2007-09-23T07:54:34Z

    Without seeing a screenshot I would imagine that the cause is that your screen is not wide enough to hold both the Dreamweaver menu items (on the left) and your menu extras such as Synergy (on the right).

    If you experiment with this you'll see that the left hand side always "wins" when there isn't enough space. Note that even if Synergy isn't visible you can still use it to control iTunes via hot keys.

    You can try to make Synergy appear further to the right (so that it is less likely to get obscured by Dreamweaver) by ensuring that it loads before other status items (that's the technical term for third-party menu add-ons). Apple-provided menu extras (Volume, Fast-User Switching etc) use a different API and will appear even further to the right.
