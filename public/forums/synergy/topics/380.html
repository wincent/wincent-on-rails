  2. Greg Hurrell 2008-08-19T10:02:46Z

    The 3.2 series is the best one for Leopard right now due to a bug in the OS that only Apple can fix. So the 3.5 alpha series is pretty much at the mercy of Apple as there's nothing I can do to fix the issue. Bug #640 is the ticket with the relevant information.

    3.2 and 3.5 are feature equivalent, so there's no penalty in using 3.2 for now. Once Apple gets its act together new features will be added to the 3.5 branch, but for now they are basically in parity with each other.

    As for the Amazon connections. It should only be connecting on those occasions when iTunes doesn't provide artwork. Have you noticed any particular pattern in which kind of songs are playing when the connections get initiated? It might be some special case in which iTunes refuses to hand over the art.
