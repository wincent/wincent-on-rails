  2. Greg Hurrell 2005-11-27T08:21:51Z

    Yes. You could type:

    Code:open /Library/PreferencePanes/Synergy.prefPane/Contents/Resources/Synergy.app

    You could even define this as an alias (shortcut) or save it as a terminal ".command" file.

    I am working on getting Synergy converted into a Universal Binary at the moment.
