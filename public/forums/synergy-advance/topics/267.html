    As at the time of writing the current version of the public preview doesn't actually stop working, but you will continue to see the reminder window if you don't register.

    From a technology perspective it's very difficult (well, impossible) to implement time-limited previews which can't be circumvented by determined crackers. So I haven't really considered spending time on developing that kind of technology to be a very good investment of my time at this stage; for now the reminder window is enough. As I get closer to version 1.0 I will tighten things up a little bit.

    You can basically wait on making a purchase decision as long as you like; after all, I want my customers to be fully satisfied with my products. I do appreciate your financial support though. It is a great help.
