Frequently Asked Questions

I've lost my serial number

Either upgrade to Synergy 6 (which is free), or obtain complimentary license details here.

Synergy won't launch at login anymore

See the "Fixing Synergy login problems" article on the wiki for a quick fix.

My serial number doesn't work

You must enter your serial number and your email address exactly as they appear on your license notice. If you modify the serial number or the email address in any way then they won't be accepted. Trying copying and pasting to minimize the risk of mistakes.

What's the difference between the different versions?

The short version:

  • Synergy 3.3 was a once-off freebie given away by MacHeist as part of their 2008 "Giving Tree" promotion.
  • Synergy 3.4 was a previous entry in the "stable" series, prior to the release of Snow Leopard. It runs on all versions of Mac OS X from 10.2.8 upwards.
  • Synergy 3.5 was an "alpha/experimental" series which is no longer actively developed. It required Mac OS X 10.5.8 or later.
  • Synergy versions in the 4.x series were a 32-bit application requiring Mac OS X 10.6 Snow Leopard or Later.
  • Synergy 5.0 is a 64-bit application that requires Mac OS X 10.8 Mountain Lion or later.
  • Synergy 6.0 is the first free version of Synergy and requires OS X 10.9 Mavericks or later.

See this blog post for further information.
